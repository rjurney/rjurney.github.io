Two Great Books on Information Visualization

 Semiology of Graphics - A classic. I’ve just ordered the new printing, but a friend lent me Stanford’s copy and I want it on my desk when I’m making diagrams of new concepts.  This is a great reference for coming up with sane and coherent symbol sets when drawing… just about anything.

 Cartographies of Time: A History of the Timeline - Inspirational.  An essential guide to the timeline.  This book taught me more about data visualization than any other, by tracing the historic origins and development of visualization from the roots of history itself right up to the modern era.
