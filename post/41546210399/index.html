Best of Pacifica

Pacifica is a small town just over the hump from the rest of the peninsula, making it a great place to live, surf, play. What follows is a guide to the best of Pacifica for new friends that have moved to the area.

  1. Mazetti’s Bakery (Flash only) - Yelp - Amazing cupcakes, pies, cookies, coffee and pastries and up at 6AM daily.
  2. Ocean Fish - Great sushi at great prices. Delivers.
  3. Bay Coffee Company - Best cup of coffee, good wifi.
  4. Pacifica Pool at Oceana Highschool - Great pool. Limited adult swim hours, but an all access pass is $67/month. Open till 8-9PM weekdays.
  5. Nor Cal Surf Shop - Great surf/bodyboarding shop. Wetsuits, boards, fins, plus great NorCal attire. Yes, this is the shop name you see everyone wearing :)
  6. High Tide Restaurant-Cafe and Creperie - great brunch. Amazing crepes.
  7. Coastside Farmers Market - The best produce, by Safeway.
  8. Oil Changers - $7 deluxe carwash with super-fun tricolor foam!
  9. Sanchez Dog Park - Great park. Bitchy neighbors.
  10. Pacifica State Beach - THE PLACE to learn to surf, bodyboard, swim. Get a lesson a NorCal Surf Shop.
